The Kessler Foundation
Understanding Spinal Cord Injury: A Course for Personal Care Assistants

The Kessler Foundation is one of the world's largest rehabilitation hospitals, dedicated to helping people with disabilities improve their quality of life. This was a grant funded project intended to create a resource for personal care assistants to give them a better understanding of Spinal Cord Injury and its impact on the care they provide.

How we helped

  • Curriculum Consulting and Organization

  • Casting and Recording Voice Talent

  • Custom Video and Photo Production, onsite and remotely

  • Organized, recorded and managed zoom interviews

  • Custom Graphic Creation

  • Thinkific Integration

  • Spanish Translation

Impact

Creation of a free, publicly available course for new and experienced personal care attendants to give them more knowledge and confidence in the care they provide to people with spinal cord injuries.

Mount Sinai Hospital Systems
SexualitySCI.org

Mount Sinai Hospital's rehabilitation department wrote a grant to host a conference about sex, love and intimacy after a spinal cord injury. We worked with them to translate the conference content into a 16-part video series and website to make the information publicly accessible online.

The Project

  • Curriculum Consulting and Organization

  • Branding and graphic creation

  • Organizing and conducting in-person interviews

  • Custom Video and Photo Production

  • Video editing

  • Custom Squarespace Website Design

  • Analytics reporting

Impact

The website recieved over 10k users from organic traffic in 2023 and gets frequent inquiries from medical professionals and people with SCI about further information and gratitude for the resource.
